Quantifier Characters
a?      zero or one of a
a*      zero or more of a
a+      one or more of a
a{3}    exactly 3 as
a{3,}   3 or more as
a{3,6}  In between 3 and 6 as


Example
        345
    +  6345
    +    76
    -------
    =  4392
